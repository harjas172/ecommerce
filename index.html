Air Jordan Retro 4 "KAWS"

Famed artist KAWS has been rather recluse since discontinuing his
streetwear brand OriginalFake, only showing face for the occasional
high art project or worthwhile collaboration. Jordan Brand commission
him for the latter; his canvas: the Air Jordan 4. The result, magnificent.
The KAWS x Air Jordan 4 features a buttery premium suede upper in smooth
and heathered textures, all drenched in a tonal grey colorway. KAWS hand
graphics are etched into the upper as well, along with the matching hang
tag. The midsole and upper “Wings” are also wrapped in suede. Finally, a
glow in the dark sole completes the look.

Air Yeezy 2 "Red October" By Kanye West


$8,500.00

Lauded for its bold tonal colorway, but also rooted in controversy,
the mega-hyped Air Yeezy 2 "Red October" by Kanye West marks the end
of his collaborative relationship with Nike. The landmark sneaker
singlehandedly created the all-red movement within the sneaker world
that's been constant since it released without warning on February 9,
2014. Ye debuted the “Red October” colorway during a live performance on
'Saturday Night Live' almost a year prior, causing a stir among talking
heads. Highly sought after and often imitated, there's no substitute for
the ubiquitous "Red October" Yeezy 2 as it marks the end an all-time
great era in sneaker history.
